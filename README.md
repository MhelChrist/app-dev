# My Favorite Anime Series
**Title:** My Hero Academia

**Creator:** Kohei Horikoshi

**Genre:** Action, Superhero, Shonen

**My Hero Academia** is a Japanese anime and manga series created by **Kohei Horikoshi**. It's about a world where most people have superpowers called **Quirks**.

The main character, **Izuku Midoriya** (also called *Deku*), is a kind and brave boy who wants to become a superhero—even though he was born without a Quirk. One day, the world’s #1 hero, **All Might**, gives Midoriya his own powerful Quirk called **One For All**.

Midoriya joins **U.A. High School**, a special school for training future heroes. There, he meets many classmates, trains hard, and fights dangerous villains especially the **League of Villains** which is led by **Shigaraki** and **All For One**.
